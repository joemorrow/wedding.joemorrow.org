Other Options

You'll also find many cute bed & breakfast and vacation rentals around the Alexander Valley, Russian River, and Dry Creek areas.

The town of Healdsburg, in particular, has a number of charming restaurants and shops centered around a picturesque downtown square. Santa Rosa is the largest nearby city; it also has a number of hotel, dining, and entertainment options.

Transportation

Curiously, taxi service is extremely limited in the Wine Country area, so you may want to consider renting or sharing a car. Please be careful if you do plan to visit any of Sonoma County's wonderful (and occasionally generous) wineries.

On Wedding Day, we're planning to offer shuttle service between the ceremony venue and the hotels listed, above. Please let us know if you're interested, so we can size our transit methods accordingly.
